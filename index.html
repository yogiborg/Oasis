The Plan:

The Oasis Subscription model will be the perfect way to live for anyone who wants to be able to work and play in any location they choose.

The Oasis Subscription Model will provide access to unoccupied Oasis Housing Units throughout the nation that can be used as either permanent or temporary living quarters, depending on your needs. All you have to do is select what type of home you want, how many people (or pets/ robots) will be staying with you, and we'll take care of the rest. If there are no available homes at your desired location, we'll simply make more. People will be charged for their current residence based on a weekly subscription model.

This model competes with traditional housing companies, hotels, and other app-based models. Subscribers can live in luxury, traveling from location to location with nothing more than essentials.

We will use artificial intelligence in every area of our company to help us grow faster, improve our services, and reduce costs for everyone. Our sustainable building practices—including geothermal heat-pumps, wind power, and solar energy—will enable us to shorten construction timeframes, lower electricity bills, and decrease maintenance costs significantly.

A microapartment would be the cheapest option available, costing no more than $500 a month. From there, the models can get more expensive depending on the needs of the customer, ranging from slightly larger apartments for larger families, to luxury townhouses decorated by community artists with costs which depend on demand and location. No matter what accommodations you’re living in though, sustainability, community, and well-being always comes first. The full suite of discounts will be available to all subscribers including access to athletic facilities and public transportation.

Eventually, we seek to convert all Americans to our living model, driving record profits for our company by reducing costs for all Americans in an increasingly remote and automated world.

The Team:

They had no money, no job, and no place to live.

But they did have a crazy idea: to create a new kind of housing and living company that caters to the needs of today's workforce.

With that, Oasis was born. Oasis is a company that provides microapartments, apartments, condos, townhouses, co-working and collaborative workspace, and value-added living services.
